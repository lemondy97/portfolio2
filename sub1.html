Already a Member?

Membership allows you to discover two million years of human history
while donating to the Louvre Museum Friends, enabling us to support
the work of the Museum. From just £75 a year (with discounts for those aged Under 26),
Membership makes visits to the Museum a cultural adventure with:

  • Free unlimited entry to exhibitions, including The world of Sphinx (until 10 october 2022),
    the Citi exhibition Feminine power: the divine to the demonic
    (until 25 December 2022) and a forthcoming show on decoding hieroglyphs.
  • An exclusive programme of Members' events.
  • Use of an exclusive Members' Room.
  • The Louvre Museum Magazine three times a year.
  • And many more benefits.

Members do not need to book for the permanent collection or our exhibitions.
You'll need to show your Membership card to gain entry. If you're visiting
before your Membership card arrives, on the day of your visit, please bring
the confirmation emails of your Membership purchase to the Membership Desk
in the Great Court. We'll then issue a temporary card to access your on-site benefits.

Read the Membership FAQs page for any queries about your visit,
or contact us at friends@Louvre museum.github

Membership benefits

Unlimited entry to exhibitions

Visit every exhibition as many times as you want.

Members' events

From out-of-hours exhibition viewings to a variety of on-site and online events.

Members' exclusive content

Explore the British Museum Magazine archive, watch special Members' YouTube videos and more.

Members' Room

Enjoy an exclusive space overlooking the Great Court.

Member discounts

10% off purchases in the Museum's shops, cafés and restaurants.

Great reason to join

Each visit to the Museum is different. I notice new things every time I return. Unlimited access to the exhibitions as a Member is great. I know I can take my time and even come back for a second (or third!) look.

Enjoy your benefits immediately

As soon as you join, you can begin enjoying the benefits of Membership.
Members do not need to book for the permanent collection or our exhibitions.
Visit the Membership Desk in the Great Court and show your Membership receipt
email and a form of identification for your temporary card to access Membership benefits straight away.

Visit again and again

The British Museum exhibitions are exceptional. As a Member, you can return to
each exhibition as many times as you want. Add a guest facility or an additional
cardholder to treat someone special and share your passion for the Museum.

Help the Louvre Museum be a museum of the world, for the world

Your Membership fee is a donation to The Louvre Museum Friends which enables us
to support the work of the Museum. From loaning objects around the France and
the rest of the world and conducting academic research,
the scope of our work couldn't be achieved without additional support.
